Our Mission

We believe access to care should never be a privilege.

The Care Box Project exists to restore dignity, comfort, and confidence through thoughtfully assembled care packages that address essential wellness and hygiene needs. Women and marginalized communities are disproportionately affected by limited access to basic care, often forced to navigate systems that overlook or undervalue their well-being.

Founded in Bellevue, The Care Box Project began as a local response to a widespread and often invisible issue.
